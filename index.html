Spinach salad

my favorite dish is spinach salad

Ingredients

  • spinach leafs
  • nuts
  • cranberries
  • croutons
  • olive oil
  • Danish blue cheese

Preparation

  1. wash and pre desinfect spinach if necessary
  2. rinse spinach and let dry
  3. spinach is placed on the plate in on orderly manner as in bed
  4. olive oil is spiced to taste on spinach
  5. dressing is added to taste on the spinach bed
  6. cranberries are scatteried over the salad
  7. scatter bits of cheese on the salad
  8. croutons are scatteered over the salad
  9. let enjoy
